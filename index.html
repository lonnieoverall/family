How many F's are their in your family?

I heard a story of a country musician who lives his life by the simple principal he calls the 3 F's

  • Faith
  • Family
  • Fan's

He bases all he does on those three factors, or principles. When presented with a choice or decision he simply asks himself if the decision lines up with these principles. When they don't line up he walks away. Sounds simple and intriguing don't you think?

What would the F's be for you and your family? For me I think they would be:

  • Faith - We are Jesus folowers and we behave as such.
  • Family - We are a family and we spend time together.
  • Fun - We do fun things together.
  • Friends - We surround ourselves with like minded people.
  • Fitness - We go outside and do things that make us sweat.
